Welcome to Pretty Planner!

Organize your entire campus life in one place.

Pretty Planner is a simple, stylish web app designed to help students stay organized in school. It lets you track daily and weekly tasks, search using powerful filters, view helpful stats like total tasks and hours spent, and keeps everything saved right in your browser— no account or server required.
